### What RTL-Gen AI Does

RTL-Gen AI transforms hardware design by:

1. **Intelligent Code Generation** - AI synthesizes optimized Verilog from high-level specifications
2. **Multi-layer Verification** - Syntax, behavioral, and correctness validation
3. **Advanced Analysis** - Power, area, timing, and synthesis analysis
4. **Pattern Learning** - Continuous improvement from design examples
5. **Enterprise Security** - Full compliance and audit trails
